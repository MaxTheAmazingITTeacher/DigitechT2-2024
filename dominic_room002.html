Option 3 The steep hill. As you carefully descend the hill,
you can make out what appears to be a stream running along the bottom.
The water looks clear enough to drink.
At this point,
you have no choice.
If you dont drink,
youll die from dehydration. You kneel next to the stream and cup some water into your hands. It tastes clean and refreshing. After a few hours you feel revitalised. Continuing along the path near the stream you see two more tracks. One leads to the mouth of the river (Option 5), the other a baron plain (Option 6).
